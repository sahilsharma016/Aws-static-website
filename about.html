About Sahil Sharma

Sahil Sharma is an AI engineer with an M.Tech in AI and Data Science. He hails from Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh, India.

Himachal Pradesh, a state in northern India, is known for its stunning Himalayan landscapes. It is famous for its hill stations, temples, and rich cultural heritage. The state offers a range of adventure sports such as trekking, climbing, and skiing. The serene environment, lush greenery, and the picturesque valleys make it a perfect destination for nature lovers and tourists seeking tranquility.
